About

It started with a boy wide-eyed and gleeful, life more crimson prostitute’s spat of fire Across the Poseidon blue water the boy’s desire’s reside. But like the traveler and the sea don’t let your hopes carry you away from reality. That’s it! The color of this boys song is Liquor Brown

Evan, Tokyo, New York City, a Man, New Jersey, Tar skin, addiction, egotistical, and a mother—these words somehow fit together, yet the feeling is indifferent all the same.

The edifice of my oeuvre exists in the space between digital gratification and self-discovery, amid a waning landscape of labels.
