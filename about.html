About

First off, I'd like thank everyone who took their time to view my page, welcome to my site. I'm Evan, born and raised in Bed-Stuy, Brooklyn - I am African American with roots in Barbados. Now, I'm expanding my horizons over at Tokyo University of Technology, where I'm not just studying, but evolving.

For storytelling I get alot of my inspiration from spike lee, Ice Cube and many other talented black storytellers, artistically i've learned a lot from many japanese visual artist and combined their skillsets to create my own. I like to express my self in a raw, unsolicited manner so I try to translate that into my works

Creativity and innovation are natural to me. I believe creativity is the last hope for the youth- whether that's through visuals, music, comedy, community work - to convey stories, challenge perceptions, and bridge divides. My mission is to create with purpose, to resonate and inspire.
