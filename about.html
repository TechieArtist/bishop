About

First off, I'd like thank everyone who took their time to view my page, welcome to my site. I'm Evan Butler, born in Bed-Stuy, Brooklyn - I am a Black American with roots tracing back to Barbados. During the decline of the Covid-19 Pandemic I spent years studying in Tokyo, Japan which heavily influenced my approach to the arts. I currently reside in Newark, New Jersey

The edifice of my art exists in the space between digital gratification and self-discovery, amid a waning landscape of labels.

-->Creativity and innovation are natural to me. I believe creativity is the last hope for the youth — whether that's through visuals, music, comedy, community work — to convey stories, challenge perceptions, and bridge divides. My mission is to create with purpose, to resonate and inspire.
